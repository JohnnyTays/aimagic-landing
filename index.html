Coach's Advice: Stage Presence
Stage presence isn't something you are born with; it is a suite of skills you inhabit. High Status performers move with purpose and aren't hurried by the audience's gaze.

The "Entitlement" Mindset: You must believe you have the absolute right to be there. This psychological shift prevents "fidgeting"—the subconscious attempt to apologize for taking up people's time.

The 3-Second Rule: Don't just scan the room; look at individuals. Hold eye contact for about three seconds—long enough to register a connection, but not long enough to make it a confrontation.
