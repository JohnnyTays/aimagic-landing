The Effect

The Method (Sleights Required)

  • Retention of Vision Vanish — The optical illusion that makes the brain see what isn't there

  • The Ramsay Subtlety — Pointing at your "empty" hand to sell the lie

Presentation & Patter

"They say wealth is fleeting... but money doesn't disappear. It travels. It follows the path of least resistance. If you watch closely, you can see the moment it loses its grip on the physical world."
